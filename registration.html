Author registration

  • Each accepted paper requires a Full IEEE member (if applicable) registration before the early registration deadline;
  • Only one paper can be associated with an individual registration;
  • Each accepted paper must be presented by one of the authors in order to appear in the proceedings and in the IEEE Xplore;
  • Register through the form in the button below;

Fees

For each accepted paper at least one author must register before May 9 2016 as Full IEEE member or Full non-IEEE member, even if this author is a student. For authors with multiple papers, one registration can cover up to two (2) papers.

Early Registration Late Registration
(until May 31 2016) (from June 1 2016)
Full non-IEEE member €500 €600
Full IEEE member* €450 €550
Student IEEE* (Attendee only) €350 €450
Student non-IEEE (Attendee only) €400 €500
Accompanying person €150 €150

*proof of student status and IEEE membership has to be provided.

Additional pages on top of the 6 included in the regular participation fee can be purchased at electronic registration page (up to 2 extra pages) for EUR 100 each. This will be accomplished when registering to the conference. As an IEEE policy, the papers by no-show authors will not be included in the IEEE Xplore.

Payment is through credit card only. All prices are in Euro (EUR). On-site registration is strongly discouraged. Accompanying persons can register for a reduced fee, but there is no special program arranged. Thus, the fee covers only the social events.
